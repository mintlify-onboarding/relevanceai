## List your datasets

Python

`from relevanceai import Client

client = Client(token=YOUR_ACTIVATION_TOKEN)

client.list_datasets()
`

## Search for datasets

This will do a string search of a dataset by its name/id.

Python

`client.search_datasets("test")
`

Alternatively, you can view and search for all your datasets at [https://cloud.relevance.ai/datasets](https://cloud.relevance.ai/datasets).
